============================================================
WHAT MAKES A WORLD CUP CHAMPION?
A Machine Learning Analysis of FIFA World Cup 1974-2022
============================================================

1. THE QUESTION
----------------------------------------
What statistical profile separates World Cup champions
from every other team in the tournament?
We analyzed 86 team-match performances across 13 World Cups.

2. THE CHAMPION DNA
----------------------------------------
Champions consistently show 4 key traits:

  TRAIT 1: CLINICAL FINISHING
  Champions convert 15.1% of shots vs 9.7%
  Shot accuracy: 47.5% vs 37.3%
  → They don't just shoot more, they score more efficiently.

  TRAIT 2: DEFENSIVE SOLIDITY
  Save rate: 93.5% vs 87.0%
  Goals conceded: 0.79 vs 1.92 per match
  Concede 0.45 fewer goals than expected (xG)
  → Champions are extremely hard to score against.

  TRAIT 3: THE CLUTCH FACTOR
  xG Overperformance: +0.19 vs -0.13
  → Champions score MORE than the stats say they should.
    Whether it's composure, star quality, or luck — they deliver.

  TRAIT 4: MATCH DOMINANCE
  Avg goal difference: +1.5 vs -0.6
  Shot dominance ratio: 1.75 vs 1.30
  Possession: 53.5% vs 48.6%
  → Champions control matches, not just survive them.

3. WHAT DOESN'T MATTER
----------------------------------------
  Discipline: Champions avg 2.67 vs 2.85
  → Virtually identical. Being 'cleaner' doesn't make you a champion.

4. ML MODEL RESULTS
----------------------------------------
  Best model: Logistic Regression
  Leave-One-Out CV Accuracy: 77.9%
  ROC-AUC: 84.7%
  Champion detection: 18/24 matches correctly identified (75%)

  Top predictive features (Random Forest importance):
    1. Shot Accuracy
    2. Goal Difference
    3. Expected Goals (xG)
    4. Stage Level (knockout rounds)
    5. Defensive xG Overperformance

5. LIMITATIONS
----------------------------------------
  - Small dataset: 43 curated matches, not every World Cup game
  - Some champions have only 1 match in the data
  - xG data for older tournaments (1974-1990) may be estimated
  - Results reflect correlation, not necessarily causation

6. FOR THE 2026 WORLD CUP
----------------------------------------
  Teams to watch should match this champion profile:
    → Convert 15%+ of shots into goals
    → Keep 93%+ defensive save rate
    → Overperform their xG (the clutch factor)
    → Maintain 53%+ possession
    → Outshoot opponents by 1.7x ratio
